\# Crossover Stockton — Vision



Crossover Stockton is a public, anonymous, mobile-first platform that routes unhoused and at-risk individuals to appropriate resources using a simple four-track system.



The platform is designed to meet people where they are, preserve autonomy, protect employment continuity, and avoid forcing users into long-term shelter or institutional pathways.



The system prioritizes speed, dignity, and real-world usability over bureaucracy or compliance-driven design.
